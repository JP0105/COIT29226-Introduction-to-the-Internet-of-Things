## Task 3: IoT and Real-world Issues  

IoT technology plays an important role in solving many real-world problems. In environmental issues, IoT sensors can monitor air quality, water levels, and weather conditions. This helps in detecting pollution and preventing natural disasters like floods. In agriculture, soil moisture sensors help farmers use water efficiently, which reduces waste and improves crop production.

From a social perspective, IoT improves healthcare through devices like fitness trackers and remote monitoring systems. Doctors can monitor patients without requiring them to visit hospitals frequently. This is especially helpful for elderly people and those living in remote areas.

In humanitarian situations, IoT can be used in disaster management. Sensors and tracking systems can help locate people, monitor conditions, and provide faster emergency response. Overall, IoT technology helps improve efficiency, safety, and quality of life in many areas.
